🙏 Our Story

A Beautiful Initiative
Born from Devotion

"Where every sweet is a prayer, and every bite carries the blessing of Radha Krishna."

We started Shree Radha Krishna Sweets with a simple but heartfelt vision — to bring the pure taste of authentic Indian Satwik sweets to the Indian community in Copenhagen. In Denmark, truly authentic homemade Indian sweets are hard to find, and we saw this as an opportunity to serve our community with love and devotion.

Every sweet we prepare is made with the finest ingredients, no onion or garlic, no artificial preservatives — purely in the Satwik tradition. From the moment we begin preparation to the final garnish, each creation is offered with devotion as a service to Shree Radha Krishna.
